Where Creativity Meets Madness

Location: jaysingpur college jaysingpur
13 April 2023



















































Welcome To

the Ad-Mad Show Event!
Year 2023

AD MAD SHOW is an ‘Advertisement Making Competition’ to showcase the creativity in marketing products and services. The event is an opportunity to bring out the individual and collective talent of the students participating in it. Its objective is to acquaint the students about promotional strategies in marketing through advertisements. It comprises humorous and conceptual ad presentations by the students. Students will act out on various advertisements script and ideas relevant for different sectors. The idea behind conducting this event is to provide a platform to participants to explore their creativity skills.

Join us for creativity and fun as we showcase the best ad-mad campaigns.

"Creativity without strategy is called art, creativity with strategy is advertising”

What makes Brands? How do they survive in this world of cutthroat competition? After all, ‘Darr kea age Jeet hai’! There’s a creator in every one of us, to explore yours, Pratyaya Club proudly presents Ad – Mad Show ‘Where creativity meets madness’.

The participants have to come up with ADs for the product they choose related to the theme given. Participants are suggested that their Ad should contain the essentials of advertisement, i.e. Clarity, provoking in nature, creativity, appeal and simplicity. The themes are

  • 1.cosmetic Products (soap,shampoo,etc)
  • 2.Food Products (fruits,vegitables,etc)
  • 3.Detergent Powder( where they advertise a service of their choice)
  • 4.cold drinks ( where they advertise a service of their choice)
  • 5.Shoes( where they advertise a service of their choice)
  • 6.online application (paytm,amazon,g-pay,etc)

Participants can come up with an ad considering ANY ONE of the above themes . It's simple and clear that he/she will have to come up with advertisements showcasing all talent and creativity they got in them. The announcement of the event will be done on our social media pages on April 7, 2023 (Wednesday). The participants will be given time until April 11, 2023 (Tuesday). Awarded to the first 3 Winners and certificates will be given to all the participants.

CRITERIA OF JUDGEMENT
  1. 1.Way of conveying the information of the product
  2. 2.Impact on the advertisement
  3. 3.Innovation and creativity
  4. 4.Content-information, Awareness and Humor
  5. 5.Appeal of the advertisement
  6. 6.Coordination
  7. 7.Overall Script and Play

GUIDELINES
  1. 1.Can be done individually or in a team ( a team can have maximum of 5 people )
  2. 2.There should be no exposure to biasness against any caste or community or religion through your performance.
  3. 3.Use of vulgar expression and language will lead to disqualification of the team.
  4. 4.The content cannot be more than 5 minutes.
  5. 5.Entery fee for team is 150/-.( per member 30/- )
